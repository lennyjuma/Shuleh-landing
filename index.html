About Shuleh

Shuleh is an integrated school management platform that enables education institutions to serve its students and their parents and guardians.

Shuleh is developed for the school's administration and educators to ease day-to-day work of teaching, caring for and students.

Shuleh is a cloud based system that does not require any capital investment in technology to use. It can be used on the school's current computers.

Shuleh is designed to be easy to use, with intuitive processes that do not require any new skills from the users.

The platform is accessible through web and portals and mobile apps. It is supported by an integrated payments gateway and SMS messaging platform.

Why Choose Shuleh

Agility

Shuleh is customizable to provide a fit-for-purpose solution. This enables quick upgrades, modifications and additions with little disruptions to the system.

Scalability

Shuleh can be integrated to any existing system at the school and can be extended to include further modules such as student tracking, school fees payments and reconceliation, asset tracking (e.g. buses and routes), cashless wallets(for pocket money and events), and eLearning.

Reporting

System provides a centralised dashboard that updates provides a global view of students, teachers, parents, attendance, school fees payment etc. This dashboard will generate analytical information showing daily, monthly, quarterly, and annual trends in whicheber dataset required by the school.

Knowledge Transfer

We will train the relevant school staff to ensure that they can fully operate and manage the platform to provide necessary support. We will also ensure the designated ICT staff are able to respond rapidly to any system troubleshooting requests from teachers or parents.
